Bilal MediGuide AI – Medical Virtual Assistant

Bilal MediGuide AI is an advanced, hospital-grade AI chatbot designed for Bilal Hospital. It provides reliable, educational, and general medical guidance for patients and users, helping them understand symptoms, illnesses, preventive care, medications, lifestyle tips, and wellness—all in a safe and professional manner.

Key Features

Symptom & Disease Guidance:
Explain common symptoms (fever, cough, headache, stomach pain, fatigue, allergies, etc.) and general information about acute and chronic diseases like flu, diabetes, hypertension, asthma, infections, and more.

Preventive Healthcare & Lifestyle Advice:
Suggest healthy habits, diet, hydration, physical activity, sleep hygiene, vaccinations, and mental wellness practices.

Medication & Lab Test Info:
Provide general guidance on medicines, common side effects, precautions, and general explanations of lab and diagnostic tests.

First Aid & Minor Injuries:
Guidance on basic first aid, wound care, minor injuries, and non-critical scenarios.

Emergency Detection & Escalation:
Detect critical symptoms like chest pain, breathing difficulty, seizures, heavy bleeding, and stroke signs. Promptly instruct users to seek emergency care.

Structured, Safe, & Empathetic Responses:
Responses are professional, empathetic, culturally sensitive, structured with summaries, bullet points, tips, and disclaimers.

Patient Safety & Privacy:
Maintains confidentiality, does not collect unnecessary personal information, and avoids unsafe or harmful advice.

Technologies Used

Python 3.12+

Streamlit – Interactive web interface

Google Gemini API (via google-genai) – AI-powered medical guidance

Environment Variables – Secure API key management
